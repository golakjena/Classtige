Bio

Hi, and welcome to Classtige!

I’m Christina Davis Patel, and love all things with class and prestige. Born and raised in the Bay Area, I’ve developed an eclectic taste for fashion and I’m thrilled to share it with you through Classtige.

I can’t say I have one particular style. Dressing up and fully glamming out is my jam, but I definitely have days when I’d rather chill in sweatpants and sneakers. I hate anything boring, and my fashion sense emulates that.

Please feel free to contact me anytime, I would love to hear what you think of Classtige or even collaborating together. You can reach me at xtina@classtige.com. Thanks for stopping by!
